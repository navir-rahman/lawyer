who we are

Honesty, credibility and experience at your service. MLGA attorneys are dedicated to providing a wide range of legal services for people who have come to the United States. With extensive experience in defending against deportation, we take the time necessary to assess the unique needs and circumstances of each client.

Our attorneys have defended clients in San Antonio, Houston, Chicago, New York, New Jersey, Pennsylvania, Atlanta, Buffalo, Cleveland and other cities. MLGA attorneys have the team of professionals you need to resolve immigration procedures. If you are looking to stay in the United States or bring your loved ones, we can help you.
